About Us

We are an international Commodity & Share trading Company situated in Dubai, UAE.

Futuristic was incorporated in December 2021 with the Dubai Multi Commodity Centre, UAE. In a short span of time, Futuristic has expanded its Share & Commodity market trading network across Middle East and U.S markets.

The company’s rapid expansion stems from the efficient, experienced, and professional members of the organization, whose key focus is to create sustainable value for our stakeholders. The culture in Futuristic places strong emphasis on fulfilling the needs of their Brokers & Customers for Physical Trade and at the same time fostering long-term relationships with them. At the core of the overall company strategy is to stay with Broker oriented and continue to diversify in trade & geographies to become a global conglomerate.

The company’s generic evolution has been an inspiration and encouragement for the entire organization. By upholding its core values strongly and capitalizing on its strengths, Futuristic will continue to pursue its vision and mission. Diversification, market expansion in Future for Other Countries Share & Commodity Trading. Our objective is to become one of the leading market share holders in one of the most competitive markets in the world.

Futuristic’s trades in

  • House and Jewellery Trading
  • Pearls & Precious Stones Trading
  • Non-Manufactured Precious Metal Trading
  • Trading for Proprietary account on regulated exchanges (DMCC)

Our team is empowered by the strong network of Various Brokers in U.A.E, Middle East and U.S. Market.
